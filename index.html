About us

Sim Racing Chicago is a professional driving simulator studio that features 10 fully built simulators. These simulators are designed to showcase the best of what sim racing has to offer. Our studio is available for any events that you may be organizing. Each of our simulators runs on iRacing's software and is configured slightly differently to provide unique experiences. With every simulator we offer, you can expect a new and exciting experience.

In addition to our professional simulators at our studio, we also offer a building service to help you create a simulator for your personal setup at home. We offer a range of options, from entry-level builds to full-motion professional rigs. During your visit to our studio, you'll have the opportunity to test the various components that make up our rigs and choose the ones that best fit your needs. We work with top brands such as Fanatec, Simucube, Apex Racing, Virtual Racing School, Heusinkveld, and many more.

Please refer to iRacing's official website for more info on the cars and tracks.

Rules and Regulations

At Sim Racing Chicago, we prioritize both your enjoyment and safety. Because our simulators are equipped with motion capabilities, we require all participants to provide a valid Driver's License or Permit. If you do not have a Driver's License or Permit, you will need to take a 30-minute class with one of our instructors. Upon completion of the class, you will receive a certificate that allows you to drive in our facility any time in the future.

To help keep our facilities clean, we ask that you wear clean sneakers while using our equipment. This will help us maintain a high level of cleanliness for all of our guests to enjoy. Thank you for your cooperation.
